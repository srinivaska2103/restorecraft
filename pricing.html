Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our billing and estimates.

Do you charge for estimates?

No, all our online and in-home estimates are 100% free with no obligation.

Do I have to pay upfront?

We require a 30% deposit to begin work and purchase materials. The remaining 70% is due upon delivery and your full satisfaction.

Is pickup and delivery included?

Yes, for all Full Restoration and Upholstery packages within a 50-mile radius, pickup and delivery are completely free.

Do you offer commercial discounts?

Yes, we offer bulk discount pricing for hotels, restaurants, and corporate offices doing 10+ items. Contact us directly for a commercial quote.
