Wood Furniture Repair

  • Crack Repair: Seamlessly filling and blending deep cracks.
  • Joint Strengthening: Re-gluing and clamping loose chair/table joints.
  • Wood Replacement: Carving exact matches for missing pieces.
  • Structural Repairs: Fixing broken legs and sagging frames.

Beyond these core services, our master craftsmen evaluate each piece's structural integrity to ensure it serves your family for generations to come. We use historically accurate materials whenever possible to preserve the original value and character of your wooden furniture.

Antique Furniture Restoration

  • Vintage Preservation: Retaining the original patina and character.
  • Traditional Finishes: Using authentic shellac and french polishing.
  • Handcrafted Detailing: Re-carving intricate lost details by hand.
  • Conservation Techniques: Halting further deterioration safely.

Our antique restoration process is carefully tailored to respect the history and value of each piece. We combine time-honored techniques with modern conservation science to breathe new life into your family heirlooms.

Upholstery & Cushion Repair

  • Fabric Replacement: Thousands of high-end fabrics to choose from.
  • Leather Repair: Re-dyeing, patching tears, and conditioning.
  • Foam Replacement: High-density memory foam upgrades.
  • Cushion Stitching: Re-stitching split seams and broken zippers.

Whether it's a beloved armchair that needs a fresh look or a dining set requiring complete re-upholstery, our skilled artisans ensure a flawless finish. We work with premium materials to guarantee comfort and durability.

Polishing & Refinishing

  • Wood Polishing: Restoring the deep, natural shine of timber.
  • Surface Refinishing: Stripping old paint and re-staining.
  • Scratch Removal: Buffing out water rings and deep gouges.
  • Protective Coating: Applying durable lacquers and polyurethanes.
  • Color Matching: Precisely matching the stain to your other furniture.

A proper finish not only enhances the beauty of wood but also protects it from everyday wear and tear. Our expert polishing services bring out the natural grain, giving your furniture a stunning, renewed appearance.
