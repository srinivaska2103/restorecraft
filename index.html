Frequent Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?
Yes, we offer fully insured white-glove pickup and delivery services within a 50-mile radius of our workshop. Fees are calculated based on distance and the size of the furniture.
How long does a typical restoration take?
A standard refinishing job usually takes 2-3 weeks. Complex antique restorations or upholstery work can take 4-6 weeks depending on material availability and the extent of the repairs.
Can you match the original stain color?
Absolutely. Our master finishers are experts in color matching. We can blend custom stains to perfectly match the original finish or coordinate with other pieces in your home.
