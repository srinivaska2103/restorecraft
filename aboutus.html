Our Story

It started in 1995 with a passion for restoring a single inherited grandfather clock. The intricate woodwork and the realization that such craftsmanship was being lost to modern mass production sparked the creation of RestoreCraft.

With decades of hands-on experience, we've grown from a single-garage hobbyist setup to a premier restoration facility.

Over the years, we have salvaged thousands of pieces from being discarded, giving them a second life. Each restored item tells a unique history, and our dedicated team of artisans treats every scratch, dent, and loose joint with the utmost respect for its heritage.

Our commitment remains the same: Quality, Sustainability, and True Craftsmanship.

Our Mission

To restore furniture with exceptional craftsmanship, extend the life of valuable pieces, and deliver outstanding customer satisfaction with every project we undertake.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted furniture restoration company, preserving both antique and modern furniture for future generations while promoting eco-friendly restoration instead of replacement.

Our Workshop

We operate a 10,000 sq ft modern restoration workshop equipped with specialized tools designed specifically for heritage preservation.

  • Dedicated dust-free wood finishing area
  • Climate-controlled polishing rooms
  • Specialized traditional hand tools
  • Rigorous quality inspection process

Our facility is designed to handle everything from delicate antiques to large commercial projects. We ensure that every piece of furniture is treated with the utmost care, utilizing both modern technology and time-honored techniques.
